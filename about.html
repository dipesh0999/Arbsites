About Us

ArB Tree Care

ArB Tree Care Ltd is a Staffordshire based Tree Surgery and Forestry Company founded by Anthony Bethell in 2002. Anthony. a forest graduate, full member of the Institute of Chartered Foresters and a registered Chartered Arboriculturist. is currently the Managing Director of the company. although he can often be found out on site with the rest of the team.VVe undertake all aspects of tree work, from dangerous tree removals and felling, tree pruning and crown reductions to tree planting, guarding and fencing. In addition to this, we provide tree and woodland management services and a consultancy services.

Some of our existing clients include members of the public, landowners and country estates owners, farmers, councils, corporate businesses, golf clubs, educational establishments, wildlife trusts and utility companies. Regardless of how big or small a contract is, we ensure that it is treated with the upmost respect and complete the job to the best of our ability, in line with BS3998 industry standards.
